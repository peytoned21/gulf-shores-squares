How It Works

The Grid

10×10 = 100 squares. 15 guys × 6 squares = 90 claimed. 10 stay blank. Numbers 0–9 randomly shuffled onto columns (winner's score) and rows (loser's score) when you lock.

Winning

After each game, take the last digit of each team's final score. Winner's last digit = column. Loser's last digit = row. Find where they intersect — that square wins $35. Blank square = $35 to slush pot.

Payout

40 games × $35 = $1,400. Every win pays $35 flat. The remaining $100 seeds the slush pot from day one. Settle up with the group tab Saturday night.

Sunday Raffle

Slush pot starts at $100 and grows with every blank hit. Sunday morning, one name gets drawn weighted by raffle tickets. Thu/Fri win = 1 ticket. Saturday win = 4 tickets. Zero wins = not in the drum.
